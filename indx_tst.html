About

The Bhaktivedanta Institute (BI) was formed in 1976 to build intellectual bridges and create joint research paths between modern empirical science and the consciousness-based metaphysical framework of India’s Bhagavata Vedanta tradition. Over the last four decades, we have steadily built science- and philosophy-based research, education and outreach programs to investigate a wide gamut of topics encompassing mind, consciousness and Vedic cosmology. The Bhaktivedanta Institute for Higher Studies (BIHS) is a recently-formulated center in Gainesville, Florida. We strongly believe that philosophy is integral to science, and that a much more holistic view of reality.
